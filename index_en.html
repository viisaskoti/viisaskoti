What does automation do for you?

Modern automation eliminates daily worries, saves money, and makes living effortless. Here are examples of what our system can do for you:

💧 Water leak monitoring (Leak detector)
  • Leak alerts: Automatic notifications to your phone if the system detects a water leak.
  • Automatic main shut-off: The system can automatically close the main water valve to minimize damage, provided a compatible shut-off device is installed.
🛡️ Security and surveillance
  • Access control and status checks: Real-time monitoring of doors and windows, and automatic notifications of unauthorized openings.
  • Camera surveillance: Smart motion detection and real-time video monitoring straight to your phone while you are away.
⚡ Electricity consumption and lighting optimization
  • EV charging: Automatic scheduling during the cheapest spot-price hours.
  • Floor heating optimization: Heating control (e.g., bathroom) based on spot electricity prices and humidity levels.
  • Smart appliance scheduling: A visual widget calculates the optimal start time (e.g., for a washing machine) based on your desired completion time and electricity prices.
  • Lighting control: Automated lighting scenes (such as motion detection only at dusk) and turning off all lights with a single press when leaving home.
  • Consumption tracking: Real-time monitoring of electricity consumption for the entire property and individual appliances.
🌡️ Indoor air, climate, and comfort
  • Indoor and outdoor monitoring: Temperature, humidity, and air quality (e.g., PM2.5, VOC, CO2).
  • Automated climate control: Automatic adjustment and management of programmed target values (such as temperature, humidity, or air quality). The system can control ventilation or heat pumps, for example, if the property has suitable and compatible equipment.
📱 No monthly fees for using the system

Using the system does not require any monthly fees paid to us – the hardware and software are entirely yours. Remote control and notifications utilize free third-party interfaces (APIs).
