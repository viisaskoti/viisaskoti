What does automation do for you?

Modern automation removes everyday worries, saves money, and makes living effortless. Here are examples of what our system can do for you:

💧 Water
  • Leak alerts: You will receive an automatic notification on your phone if the system detects a water leak.
  • Automatic main shut-off: The system can close the main water valve in the event of a leak to minimize water damage, even if you are not at home.
🛡️ Security
  • Access control: Real-time monitoring of door and window status, and automatic notifications to your phone in case of unauthorized opening.
  • Video surveillance: Smart motion detection and live video feed from the camera directly to your phone.
⚡ Electricity and heating
  • Spot price optimization: The system automatically schedules EV charging and appliance usage during the cheapest hours of the day.
  • Underfloor heating control: Smart heating adjustment (e.g., in the bathroom) based on electricity prices and humidity levels, without compromising on comfort.
  • Lighting automation: Lights turn on via motion detection only at dusk, and you can turn off all the lights in your home with a single press when leaving.
  • Consumption monitoring: View real-time electricity consumption for the entire property and individual appliances directly in the app.
🌡️ Indoor air, climate, and comfort
  • Indoor and outdoor monitoring: Temperature, humidity, and air quality (e.g., PM2.5, VOC, CO2).
  • Automated climate control: Automatic adjustment and management of programmed target values (such as temperature, humidity, or air quality). The system can control ventilation or heat pumps, for example, if the property has suitable and compatible equipment.
📱 No monthly fees for using the system

Using the system does not require any monthly fees paid to us – the hardware and software are entirely yours. Remote control and notifications utilize free third-party interfaces (APIs).

How does our solution differ from others?

There are plenty of smart home solutions on the market, but we offer something completely different – independence from cloud services and the freedom to choose devices from various manufacturers.

🌐 Fully local management

Our system runs directly inside your home without requiring an internet connection. The internet is only needed if you want the system to display the current weather at your cottage, fetch spot electricity prices, or if you are traveling and want to check on your home remotely.

☁️ No dependence on cloud services

Most modern systems operate through the cloud. For example, when you turn on an alarm, the command first goes through a foreign server. If the internet disconnects or the cloud service crashes, the system stops working.

In our solution, all commands are executed locally inside your home in a completely offline mode. However, if needed, a secure remote connection can be configured for the system, implemented in full compliance with generally accepted cybersecurity practices.

🔌 Independence from device manufacturers (Freedom to choose)

Our system does not tie you to one specific brand. You are free to buy, for example, a Philips smart bulb and pair it with a smart switch from another manufacturer – in our system, they will communicate with each other seamlessly.

The main criterion is that the devices support the open standards and technologies (such as Zigbee, Matter, or Wi-Fi) on which the system is built in your home. We are always happy to help you select the right and compatible devices.

Do you already have Wi-Fi devices in use?
It is also possible to integrate existing Wi-Fi devices into our system. However, since there are many different Wi-Fi device manufacturers on the market, we are happy to check the compatibility of these devices on a case-by-case basis before starting the installation.

Reliable technologies without renovation worries

The core of our system is built upon the flexible, open-source openHAB platform. This allows for the seamless combination of various technologies and guarantees that the system adapts flexibly to your house's structure – without the need for major renovations.

Modern wireless freedom We primarily use Zigbee and Matter standards. Most sensors run on batteries for years, so there is no need to run cables, and the installation will not affect any renovations already completed.
*Note: certain devices (such as valve motors or heavy-duty relays) may require a local power supply.
Ready for professional-grade systems The system adapts even to demanding properties. We can also integrate industrial and automation standards into the solution, such as Modbus / ModbusTCP, KNX, or LonWorks.
Customized hardware support Do your premises have specialized equipment? If needed, we are ready to implement a customized hardware and software solution specifically for your equipment under a separate agreement, ensuring it becomes part of a unified automation system.

Modern and Intuitive User Interface

The automation systems we implement are not complex to manage. You will have access to a clean and visually elegant user interface that allows you to monitor and control the status of your home or property in real time from any device—whether it's a phone, tablet, or computer.

Spot Electricity Price Information
A dedicated widget displaying electricity price data, through which the system tracks market spot prices in real time.

With compatible hardware, it is possible to automatically shift electrical loads to the most cost-effective hours, for example:

  • Charging an electric vehicle
  • Turning underfloor heating on/off
  • Turning the water heater on/off
  • Controlling any other preferred electrical load
Camera Surveillance and Local Storage (ONVIF)
The system supports ONVIF-compliant IP cameras, enabling broad device compatibility across different manufacturers.

Key advantages of this solution:

  • Motion Detection and Notifications: Based on motion detection, the system can record events locally to the server and send instant notifications to the mobile app.
  • Privacy and Cost Efficiency: Recording is handled entirely without mandatory cloud subscriptions, which enhances privacy and eliminates ongoing operational costs.
Home Appliance Scheduling Automation
A smart widget that calculates the optimal start time for high-consumption appliances (such as washing machines, dishwashers, or tumble dryers) based on spot electricity prices.

You simply specify the program duration and the deadline by which the cycle must be finished, and the system tells you:

  • How many minutes to wait before starting
  • What delay (timer) should be set on the home appliance so it runs during the cheapest hours
Security System – Overview
Visual real-time status of the property's overall security. The system shows at a glance whether everything is in order or if a device or sensor has triggered an alert.
Precise Targeting and Diagnostics
The alarm view immediately shows you exactly which of the installed sensors triggered the alert. This allows the issue to be localized and resolved without delay at the correct spot.
Mobile Notifications and Personalization
The system can send push notifications tailored to predefined situations and specific needs. This can be a critical alert message or any other preferred status update delivered directly to your phone.
  • 🎨 Custom Visual Style at No Extra Cost: You can provide your own icons (matching our technical requirements), and we will implement them free of charge.
  • ✍️ Tailored Messages: You can select and customize the text content of your notifications entirely according to your preferences—this is also included in the service.
Weather Information and Forecasts
Displays current weather data and forecasts. Local weather conditions are optimized and adapted precisely to the location of your home, cabin, or any other preferred site.
Historical Data and Analytical Charts
An example of historical data collected by the system for weather and temperature.
  • Comprehensive history charts and graphs are available for almost any metric, including temperatures, air humidity, device statuses, and electricity consumption.

ℹ️ Note on Real-Time Data:
The functionality of smart widgets, electricity price updates, and real-time synchronization of weather forecasts require a continuous and active internet connection at the property. The system utilizes official, free third-party APIs (such as the Finnish Meteorological Institute FMI and spot electricity pricing servers), whose availability and update speeds depend entirely on those service providers' own network services.
